INTRODUCTION

We know that the market trend is digitization and automation, and Commerce is one of the fields that shows great growth, e-commerce has been on the rise for years and the 2019 crisis has further accelerated its penetration among consumers. One of the great advantages of this method is the reach it has to the public since with a web page, you will be automatically accessible from all over the world (although you will always have to take into account which countries you can send to and which ones you cannot), in addition This allows us to have lower costs, since we would avoid certain expenses that a physical store incurs. This makes entry barriers much lower and better profit margins can be achieved. One of the features that we implement is 24-hour service, both with automated service by boots and by professional people.

HEALTH

Over the years, technology and human development have made it possible to study a wide variety of viruses, bacteria, and other organisms in much greater depth than in previous years, allowing us greater access to information about certain diseases that afflicted humanity, also allows us to have a strong health system; which is a set of entities and social organizations that are responsible for the production of health services, which aim to improve or protect the health of human beings. one of the most common measures to achieve this goal are medicines that in most cases, they are used to cure, slow down, prevent diseases or relieve symptoms and could even help us diagnose some diseases. Advances in medicine have made it possible for doctors to cure many diseases and save many lives. Today, many medications are obtained from a wide variety of sources. Many of which come from substances found in nature, such as plants. Other medicines are made in laboratories, mixing a series of chemical substances. In almost all cases, these medicines require careful planning in terms of distribution and care, since certain medicines require a special environment to maintain their medical qualities, on the other hand, certain medicines are not very demanding in terms of distribution and care. The distribution of medicines and pharmaceutical products consists of obtaining, preserving and delivering these products to the final customer; Many agents are involved in this process, starting with a laboratory and ending with the end customer. These agents, by working in harmony, allow this chain to fulfill its objective.

SERVICE

We know that on our site people can have access to a wide variety of pharmaceutical products to satisfy their needs, the page presents a simple and intuitive format that will allow people to interact with it in an easy and fast way, we know that many could have many doubts regarding the means of payment, deliveries, delivery times, protocols and even restrictions of certain medications, for this we have support advisors to be able to answer the queries of people who wish to purchase a product from the platform, we seek that people feel comfortable when entering and interacting with the page, avoiding showing intrusive and annoying advertisements, with which we seek to generate trust and comfort to the public and thus have their acceptance through the purchase of our products. One of the advantages that our page presents is the simplicity, in addition to having the support of professionals in the corresponding area, which are required to attend in a personalized way the requirements of each person, we seek a more humanized attention, avoiding unnecessary abuse of boots at the time of receiving any requirement from the client. we want to contribute to our society by providing quality products at an affordable price, serving in the best possible way.
