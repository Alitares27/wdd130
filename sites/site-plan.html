Purpose

I have worked for many years in logistics, I am a business administrator focused especially on logistics and supply chain; I worked in several companies and in many areas related mainly to logistics, these companies are focused on retail, I would like to automate, make the process of selling and distributing medicines more accessible and simpler, creating an online platform, in which the small pharmacies of the city can come together to meet the demand for medicines. who want be healthy and enjoy with his or her family of this blessing? Everyone!!! everyone wants to be healthy and we know that health is very important to all of us, that's why we want to bring these resources closer to all the people who need and want them, bringing medicines to where they are.

<>

Audience

This project is aimed at all the people in the city who require recognized and proven quality medicines, it is especially aimed at those people who have serious challenges in finding medicines, either because of the distance or because of the schedules to find an available pharmacy in the city. city. We want to satisfy the demand of the city's population that is looking for quality medicines at low prices to treat the different ills related to health present in society.
